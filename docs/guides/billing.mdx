## Plans

| Plan | Price | Credits/Month | Rate Limit |
|------|-------|--------------|------------|
| Free | $0 | 500 | 5 req/s |
| Starter | $19 | 10,000 | 50 req/s |
| Pro | $99 | 100,000 | 200 req/s |
| Enterprise | Custom | Custom | Custom |

## Overage

If you exceed your plan's monthly credits, you can enable auto-recharge:

- **Starter**: $1.00 per additional 1,000 credits
- **Pro**: $0.50 per additional 1,000 credits
- **Enterprise**: $0.25 per additional 1,000 credits

Overage is billed at the end of your billing cycle.

## Invoicing

All plans are billed monthly via Stripe. Invoices are emailed automatically and
available from the dashboard. Enterprise plans support annual billing and PO
invoices.

## Cancellation

You can cancel anytime. Your service continues through the end of the current
billing period. No refunds for partial months.
